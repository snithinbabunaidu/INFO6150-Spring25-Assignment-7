About Luna

Luna is an extraordinary 3-year-old Border Collie with exceptional intelligence and agility. She has mastered over 30 different commands and tricks, making her a standout competitor in the "Smartest" and "Best Tricks" categories.

Her owner, Emma, has been training Luna since she was just 8 weeks old. Their bond is incredible, allowing them to communicate almost telepathically during performances. Luna's ability to learn new tricks in just a few training sessions has amazed professional dog trainers.

When not competing, Luna enjoys herding balls in the backyard, going on hikes, and playing frisbee at the local dog park. She's also a therapy dog who visits nursing homes twice a month, bringing joy to residents with her tricks and gentle demeanor.

Special Skills

  • Advanced frisbee catching
  • Mathematical problem solving (can add numbers up to 10)
  • Object recognition (can identify 50+ different items by name)
  • Agility course navigation
  • Advanced fetch strategies (can retrieve specific toys by name)
